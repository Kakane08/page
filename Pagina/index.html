About Us

GhostDrewker is formed by a small artist, who as a hobby is dedicated to draw using the traditional method of pencil and paper.
My goal is to make my art known to the public and to make myself known.
Everything is completely made with just a sheet of paper and a pencil, a lot of imagination and references.
